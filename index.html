What is Vinesauce

Vinesauce is a collective of online streamers first created by Vincent Franzetti, also known as Vincent "Funny Calzone Man" Pizzapasta or Binyot in 2010. The Vinesauce team streams a variety of content. The cover many genres of Videogames, Music, movies, and fan created content. Their purpose is to entertain and make their fans laugh. The Vinesauce team holds a charity event every summer; the team streams for 24 hours straight; they call this event “Vinesauce is Hope.”
