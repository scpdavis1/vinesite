Mario's Mystery Meat

Storyline

Mario's Mystery Meat is a ROM hack of Super Mario World made by Vinesauce viewer "Eminus". This game takes all of the best Vinesauce references and mashes them into a story that follows Sponge as he goes on an adventure to find meat for spaghetti.

Mario and Luigi have an endless supply of spaghetti. However, they always end up lacking meat.

Level Transitions

Level transitions are a big part of what make this a unique experience. Here is a list of Level transitions. navigate to an image for a more in depth description.

The first transition. Entering the meat. Will finding meat for Spaghetti be your only quest?

After a short quiz with the 5 headed duck, you enter a closet to the Smile ghost living in meat.

You fall through howard the duck...

You exit meat you entered in the beginning via Mr. Dinks spaghetti.

After the Earthbound segment, you enter the spaghetti chasing the meat you just exited, all for your spaghetti.

Ending the spaghetti zone leads to a point and click childrens game interface. We think the meat is causing visual hallucinations.

This level is in itself a transition. You cannot win. You are chased by fire until the bitter end, which ends up being nothing but a horrible nightmare for Sponge.

Plot line change, after you exit Chatland, you have to fight their invading army and save their princess. The transition is in the form of a image slideshow.

Finishing the UN Squadron section and defending Chatland brings you to your final task, save the princes by finding and destroying vineRizon and meat...
